About konoha

History of the Hidden Leaf

Founded by the Senju and Uchiha clans, the Hidden Leaf Village was the world's first hidden shinobi village. Governed by a leader known as the Hokage , it has survived countless wars and devastating attacks by always rebuilding stronger. Today, its ninja remains fiercely united by the Will of Fire —the ultimate belief that love and loyalty protect the village.

The Hokage Legacy

The Hokage (Fire Shadow) is the supreme leader of the Hidden Leaf Village, chosen as the ultimate protector of its people. From the legendary founding of Hashirama Senju to generations of brave successors, each Hokage leaves a lasting mark by defending the village through major wars, guiding it through times of peace, and passing down the Will of Fire to the next generation.

Join the Ninja Academy

Step onto the path of a true shinobi! At the Hidden Leaf Academy, young recruits master the fundamentals of Ninjutsu, Genjutsu, and Taijutsu under master instructors. Train your chakra, forge unbreakable bonds with your squad, and prepare to protect the village with the Will of Fire.

Ninja Ranks & Regulations

All Shinobi operating within Kohonagakure must adhere to the Shinobi Code. From Academy Student to Genin, Chunin, and Jonin, every ninja serves under the direct authority of the Hokage to ensure the safety and prosperity of the Hidden Leaf Village.
